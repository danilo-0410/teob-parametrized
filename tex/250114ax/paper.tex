\title{Tests of General Relativity with GW250114}

\begin{abstract}
    We present a suite of tests of general relativity on the gravitational wave signal
    GW250114, the loudest gravitational wave signal detected by the LIGO-Virgo-Kagra
    collaboration to date. We do this, that and the other. We show that we are the best.
    We also include eccentricity and precession, because we can.
    We find that the signal is consistent with general relativity, and we rule out
    alternative theories of gravity with a high confidence level.
\end{abstract}
